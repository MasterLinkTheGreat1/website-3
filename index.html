What is Liquidity

Liquidity is how close your assests are to currency. So cash will be maximum liquidity, this is also know as M1 liquidity. The next step, M2, is near money. This contains savings account and CDs.

What does MZM mean?

MZM means Money Zero Maturity, it mesures all money that is readily availble this means cash in hand or money in a checking account, but money in a CD or in a savings account wont be counted becuase it is in the bank.

Functions of Money

Unit of Account

Unit of account is a monetary unit of measurement of the market value of goods, services, and other transactions

Store of Vaule

A store of value is an asset, commodity, or currency that maintains its value without a loss in vaule.

Medium of Exchange

The medium of exchange is the used to facilitate the purchase and sale of goods and services between parties.
