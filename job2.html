What are Government Economists?

Government economists collect and analyze data about the United States and use that information to advise on policies and projects. These members work in the MACRO part of our economy. One of these members is Jerome Powell.

Jerome Powell

Jerome Powell is an investment banker who has been with the government since 2018 as the 16th chair of the Federal Reserve. Mr. Powell also serves as Chairman of the Federal Open Market Committee, a monetary policy body. Prior to his current stay on the Board, Mr. Powell was working at the Bipartisan Policy Center in Washington, D.C., where he focused on federal and state fiscal issues.
