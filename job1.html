Business Economists

Business Economists are workers who are in applied economics. They use economic theory and quantitative methods to analyze business economics.

People in Business Economics

There have been many business economists. One famous one is John Maynard Keynes. In the 1930s, the Great Depression was causing huge problems for the economy. He was one of the main leaders who helped end the depression in Europe. He argued that aggregate demand determined the overall level of economic activity. He found out that inadequate aggregate demand could lead to prolonged periods of high unemployment, preventing economic growth.
